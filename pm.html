My Journey into Project Management

  • Experience: Managed over 40 infrastructure projects of various sizes — many of them running simultaneously -- 2022-2024

  • CSM® Certified: Built on my Agile/Hybrid knowledge and passed the Certified Scrum Master (CSM®) -- 2025
  • Product Manager role: Product manager role working for a Canadian health insurance company, using Agile to manage and deliver digital products (Web and Mobile) -- 2025-2026
