My Journey into Project Management



Here are some milestones in my Project/Product journey:

  • Mentorship: from an experienced project mamager at IBM.
  • Formal Education: Completed a one-year Project Management certificate at the University of Toronto - School of Continuing Studies.
  • First PM Role: Stepped into my first real assignment as an Associate Project Manager, supporting a strategic client initiative.
  • Promotion: Landed my first official PM role in a fast-paced environment supporting an oil & energy company.
  • Major Win: Led a two-year, enterprise-level project from start to finish.
  • Experience:
    • Managed over 40 infrastructure projects of various sizes — many of them running simultaneously.
  • PMP® Certified: Passed the PMP® exam with above target scores in all three domains on December 2, 2024.
  • CSM® Certified: Built on my Agile/Hybrid knowledge and passed the Certified Scrum Master (CSM®) exam on January 19, 2025.
  • Product Manager role: Started on my first role as a product manager working for a health insurance company.
