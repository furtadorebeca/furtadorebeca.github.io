In 2025, I enrolled back, but not until I took Luke Barousse's and Kelly Adam's course Python for Data Analytics. The course gave me the confidence and the courage to return. Data Analysis of the data job Market - Project

Analysis of the data job market, focusing on data analyst roles. This project was the result of taking the Python for Data Analytics course, created by Luke Barousse and Kelly Adams. The objective was to navigate and understand the job market for data-related positions more effectively. This project delves into the top-paying and in-demand skills to help find optimal job opportunities for data analysts.
